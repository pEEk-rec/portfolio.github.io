BFS for Waste Collection and Management

City Waste Collection Graph

The city is represented as a graph where:

  • Nodes: Waste collection points (neighborhoods, blocks, or facilities).
  • Edges: Roads or paths connecting these points.
  • Edge Weights: Represent travel time or distance (not used in BFS).
