🚀 **Project Overview**

The project explores three core business cases:

1. **Tidal Energy Production Plant**

-Objective: Harness renewable energy from ocean tides to generate electricity sustainably.
-Scope: 1.Analyze tidal flow data and simulate energy output.
        2.Assess cost-efficiency and environmental impact.
        3.Present findings through simulations and visualizations.
  
2. **Ports and Harbors Development**

-Objective: Design sustainable and efficient port infrastructure to support coastal economies.

-Scope:
       1.Optimize port layouts for enhanced logistics.
       2.Incorporate eco-friendly construction practices.
       3.Evaluate strategies to mitigate environmental disruption.
      
3. **Electric Transportation – Buses**
   
-Objective: Transition coastal regions to electric public transportation systems.
-Scope:1.Assess the feasibility of implementing electric buses.
       2.Study charging infrastructure requirements.
       3.Model cost savings and environmental benefits.
