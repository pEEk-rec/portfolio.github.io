Introduction

Welcome to Team Globalia's Portfolio! We are a group of Computer Science students collaborating on the Smart City Project in Goa. Our mission is to develop innovative solutions that combine cutting-edge technology, efficient algorithms, and sustainable practices. By addressing real-world challenges such as energy optimization, cultural heritage preservation, and transportation systems, we aim to contribute to the development of smarter, more sustainable urban environments. Our work reflects a blend of creativity, technical expertise, and a commitment to creating a better future through technology

About

This portfolio showcases our work on various aspects of the Smart City Project, including transportation systems, cultural heritage preservation, and tidal energy solutions. Each team member has contributed unique perspectives and technical skills to develop sustainable and impactful solutions.

Our portfolio includes:

  • Reflections on our learning journey during the project.
  • Business case studies highlighting the practical applications and impact of our solutions.
  • Algorithm implementations (such as Dijkstra's and Quick Sort) to optimize resource allocation.
  • Links to GitHub repositories for code and documentation, providing a deeper look into our technical work.

Through this portfolio, we aim to present a blend of creativity, analytical thinking, and teamwork that defines Team Globalia. We invite you to explore our work and join us in our vision for smarter, greener cities.
